Study in GERMANY

Known for an almost-free university education, Germany is home to the third-best education system only after the United States and the United Kingdom. With more than 400 courses and degrees offered in English, it has emerged as the third-most popular study destination for international students. Germany is the most popular non-anglophone study destination. It boasts 426 world-class universities that attract more than 370,000 international students every year.
