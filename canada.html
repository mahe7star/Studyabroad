Study in CANADA

More than 800,000 international applicants choose to study abroad every year. Lately, many such students are choosing to study in Canada over other countries. As per a survey conducted by American Association of Collegiate Registrars and Admissions Officers, a decline of 39% international applications have been noticed in 250 American Universities. On the other hand, there has been tremendous increase in number of international applications at top universities in Canada.
